Online tickets services

Making a queue for buying match ticket is simply waste of precious time.
If you are planning to watch the most awaited match of the day don't even hope to get a ticket.

Online ticket booking platform helps users to get tickets very easly and fast by knowing the availability of tickets and seats.
It will make them feel secure and seat comfortably according to the places number on their tickets, and wait the real time of the event.

The communication is stable 24/7, because you will not have to worry about reaching on booking your ticket.

How does the website work?

Main important features(core)

A user will have to log in in order to access the website after logging in he/she will select a match to attend.

By selecting an event, a user will book a ticket that will contain an id-number,a seat-number and the price.
